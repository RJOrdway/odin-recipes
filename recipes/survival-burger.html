Survival Burger

Stardew Valley

Not everything with a bun is a burger, or appetizing

Stardew Valley describes this dish as follows:

"A convenient snack for the explorer."

Personally, I need to know what a cave carrot is before I order one of these

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Eggplant
  • Cave Carrot

Seriously, what is this thing??

Steps

  1. Have Robin build the first house expansion for you
    You may need this to create ingredients and definitely to create the dish
    Since it takes 3 days for her to finish it, you should start here
  2. Acquire ingredients:
    • Bread can be bought at the saloon or made from wheat flour in your kitchen
    • Eggplants must be grown in fall, though you might get one off the travelling merchant at a steep price
    • Cave carrots can be found in caves (as you might expect)
  3. Whip up as many of these as you want in your brand new kitchen!
